About Us

Villagers Cafe is a modern village-theme cafe located at Hal Gate Korwa, Amethi. Inspired by the vibe of youth cafes and chai culture, we created a place where people can relax, enjoy tasty food, click aesthetic pictures and make unforgettable memories. From hot coffee to delicious fast food, Villagers Cafe brings together taste, ambience and happiness.
